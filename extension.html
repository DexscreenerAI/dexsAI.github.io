📥 Installation (Developer Mode)

1

Download the Extension

Click the download button above to get the dexscreener-extension.zip file.

2

Extract the ZIP

Right-click the downloaded file → Extract All → Choose a location (e.g., Desktop).

3

Open Chrome Extensions

Open Chrome and go to: chrome://extensions/

4

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner.

5

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder (where you see manifest.json).

6

Done! 🎉

Go to dexscreener.com and click on any token. The AI panel will appear!

🚀 Publish on Chrome Web Store

Want to make your extension available to everyone? Here's how to publish it:

1. Create a Developer Account

Go to Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard and sign in with your Google account. Pay the one-time $5 registration fee.

2. Prepare Your Assets

You'll need: Icon (128x128 PNG), Screenshots (1280x800 or 640x400), Description, and promotional images.

3. Create a ZIP Package

Make sure your extension folder contains: manifest.json, content.js, content.css, and icons/ folder.

4. Upload to Dashboard

Click "New Item" in the Developer Dashboard, upload your ZIP, fill in all required fields (title, description, category).

5. Submit for Review

Click "Submit for Review". Google will review your extension (usually 1-3 business days). You'll get an email when it's approved.

6. Share Your Link! 🎉

Once approved, you'll get a Chrome Web Store link like:
https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/your-extension-id

⚠️ Important Requirements

Google requires: Clear privacy policy, accurate description, no misleading claims, and your extension must provide real value. Make sure to test everything before submitting!
