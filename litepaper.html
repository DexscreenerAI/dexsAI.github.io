Powered by Claude AI

Our analysis engine uses Claude AI by Anthropic, one of the most advanced large language models available. This isn't a simple rule-based system — it's genuine artificial intelligence that understands context, identifies patterns, and provides nuanced analysis.

The AI analyzes multiple data points: price action, market cap, liquidity, trading volume, holder distribution, token age, buy/sell ratios, and on-chain security data from Rugcheck. It then synthesizes this into actionable insights.

This premium AI costs us real money to operate — but we offer it completely free to users. That's our commitment to the community.
